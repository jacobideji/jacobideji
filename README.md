#### What I'm building

* AI IR Overlay™ is a practical incident response framework that enables teams to securely deploy and manage AI agents in production. It is based on NIST SP 800-61 r3 and aligned with NIST AI RMF, NIST CSF 2.0, OWASP Top 10 for Agentic Applications 2026, ISO/IEC 42001, and the EU AI Act. The framework is open source under the Apache 2.0 license. Version v0.6.x includes seven released playbooks, with seventeen more in draft for future release.
* Book: Field Manual for Cyber Incident Handling and Response: Prevent. Respond. Recover. (co-authored with Austin Weedor). Available in paperback, Kindle, and Audible formats.
* LinkedIn Newsletter: AI IR Overlay. Twenty-four issues address agent identity risk, emerging evidence sources, and governance considerations for authorized actors.
